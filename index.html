Natalie Chan

1st Year MIMS Student at UC Berkeley

I am a first year MIMS student focusing on UX design. I graduated from the University of Edinburgh in 2020 with a degree in Architecture. With my prior background in Architecture, I am particularly interested in exploring the intricate relationship between digital technologies, human experience, and spatial design.

As a designer, I enjoy working in various design disciplines (i.e. architecture, installation, graphic, and 3D design). I aspire to continue to grow as a multi-disciplinary designer that holistically researches and designs for both the digital and physical user experience. I value sensory experience, social inclusiveness, and sustainability.
